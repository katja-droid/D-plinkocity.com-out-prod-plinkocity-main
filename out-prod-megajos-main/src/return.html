Return Policy

At Megajos.com, your satisfaction is paramount. If you are dissatisfied with your purchase, we offer clear guidelines for returns. Here are the steps and rules to assist you:

1. Return Eligibility:

Items must be in their original condition for a return: unworn, with original tags attached, and in their initial packaging. Items that are customized or personalized are returnable only if they arrive defective or damaged.

2. Time Frame for Returns:

Returns must be initiated within 14 days of receipt. Please contact our customer service promptly to start the return process.

3. How to Return:

Email our support team to obtain a return authorization. Securely repackage the product in its original packaging, including all accessories, and label it with the return authorization number. Return the item using a traceable shipping method. The customer bears return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

4. Processing Refunds and Exchanges:

Upon receiving and inspecting your return, we will process your refund or exchange. Refunds are credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. Exchanges depend on inventory availability; if unavailable, a refund or a replacement may be offered. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless it is our mistake.

5. Handling Damaged or Incorrect Items:

If you receive an item that is damaged or incorrect, please contact our customer service immediately with photographs and a detailed account of the issue. We will handle return shipping costs for such items and provide a refund or replacement as needed.

6. Items Not Eligible for Return:

Some items, such as undergarments, cannot be returned due to hygiene concerns. Check the specific product descriptions for more details or reach out to our customer support for information.

7. Exceptions to Return Policy:

Items bought from external vendors or third-party sellers are not returnable through our platform. Check the return policies of these sellers before making a purchase.

For additional help or questions about our return guidelines, please feel free to reach out to our customer support team at info@Megajos.com. We are here to ensure your complete satisfaction and to provide outstanding customer service.
