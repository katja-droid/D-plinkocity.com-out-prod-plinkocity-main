Privacy Commitment

At PlinkoCity, we prioritize your privacy and the security of your personal information. This document explains our commitment to protecting your data and the practices you agree to by using our services and website.

1. Collection of Information:

Personal Data: We collect essential information such as your name, email, postal address, and payment details during transactions.

Interaction Data: We track your interactions with our website using technologies like cookies, capturing details like your IP address and browser type.

2. Use of Information:

Transaction Processing: Your personal data helps us process and fulfill orders, and maintain communication during transactions.

Communication: We use your contact details to send order confirmations and updates.

Customization: With your consent, we customize your browsing experience based on your past interactions and preferences.

Service Improvement: We analyze the data collected to continuously improve our website and services.

3. Disclosure of Information:

Business Operations: We share your data with third-party service providers under strict privacy agreements for logistical and payment processing support.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law to protect our rights, secure our services, or ensure public safety.

4. Security Measures:

We implement security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and misuse, recognizing that online information transmission has inherent risks.

5. Your Privacy Choices:

Marketing Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time using the links provided in our messages or by contacting us directly.

Cookies: You have the ability to manage your cookie settings through your web browser.

6. Child Safety:

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 16. If such information is inadvertently gathered, it will be removed from our systems immediately.

7. Policy Updates:

Our Privacy Policy is updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review it regularly. Continuing to use our services after an update indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

For any questions or further information about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@PlinkoCity.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns and upholding your privacy rights efficiently.
